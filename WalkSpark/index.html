Turn Every Step into a Spark of Achievement

WalkSpark is an innovative fitness app that transforms your daily walking routine into an exciting challenge. By tracking your steps, you earn energy points that propel you up the daily leaderboard, motivating you to stay active and achieve your fitness goals.

Step Tracking

Accurately tracks every step you take throughout the day using your device's built-in sensors and Apple Health integration.

Energy Conversion

Converts your steps into energy points that you can use to boost your position on the daily leaderboard.

Daily Leaderboards

Compete with friends and other users on daily leaderboards to stay motivated and push your limits.

Personalized Goals

Set custom step goals based on your fitness level and gradually increase them as you improve.

Achievement Badges

Earn badges and rewards as you reach milestones and consistently meet your daily step goals.

Health Insights

Get valuable insights into your activity patterns and progress over time with detailed statistics.

How WalkSpark Works

Download

Download WalkSpark from the App Store and create your profile.

Walk

Start walking! The app automatically tracks your steps throughout the day.

Earn Energy

Convert your steps into energy points that power your position on the leaderboard.

Compete

Use your energy to climb the daily leaderboard and challenge friends.
