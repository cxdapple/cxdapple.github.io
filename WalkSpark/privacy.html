WalkSpark Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-04-19

JSBTec ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our WalkSpark app (the "App"). By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Apple Health Data

When you use the WalkSpark App, we may request access to your Apple Health data. This includes information such as your steps, distance walked, and other physical activity data. We collect this information to provide you with personalized features and statistics related to your exercise. The data is stored securely on our servers and is used solely for the purpose of enhancing your experience within the App.

1.2 Device ID

We collect your device ID for the purpose of providing personalized advertising. This identifier helps us understand your device type and preferences, allowing us to display ads that are more relevant to you. We do not link your device ID to any personally identifiable information without your explicit consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the App's functionality, such as calculating your exercise statistics and providing personalized recommendations.
  • To display personalized advertisements that are more likely to be of interest to you.
  • To conduct research and analysis to enhance our services and develop new features.
  • To communicate with you about the App, including updates, new features, and promotional offers.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • With our service providers who assist us in operating the App, such as cloud storage providers and analytics companies. These third parties are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are only permitted to use your information for the purposes for which we have engaged them.
  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or government requests.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

You can control the information we collect and how it is used in the following ways:

  • You can revoke access to your Apple Health data at any time through your device's settings.
  • You can opt - out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's privacy settings or by using the "opt - out" feature within the App.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the App or by sending you an email. Your continued use of the App after the changes are posted will constitute your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
